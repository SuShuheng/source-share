### 1. Anthropic 与 OpenAI 同日宣布企业AI合资公司，争夺企业市场

**来源**: TechCrunch | [原文链接](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/04/anthropic-and-openai-are-both-launching-joint-ventures-for-enterprise-ai-services/)
